Privacy Policy

Last updated - June 2026

Your privacy is fundamental

Dayfold is built on a simple principle: your days are yours. Everything you put into Dayfold — your schedule, habits, notes, journal entries, the birthdays you track — stays private by default. We don't sell your data, track you across the web, or use it to build advertising profiles.

What we collect

When you use Dayfold, we collect:

  • Account information: Your email address and password (encrypted) so you can log in
  • Your content: All the data you create in Dayfold (habits, schedule, tasks, notes, journal entries)
  • Device information: Device type, OS version, app version for bug fixes and improvements
  • Usage analytics: Which screens you use, feature adoption (aggregated, never tied to your personal data)

What we don't do

  • We don't show ads or track you across apps and websites
  • We don't sell or share your personal data with third parties
  • We don't use your content to train AI or build profiles about you
  • We don't access your photos, location, contacts, or calendar unless you explicitly share them

Where your data lives

Your data is stored on secure servers. We use industry-standard encryption (TLS) for data in transit and encryption at rest. Backups are encrypted and stored in geographically separate locations.

Your rights

You can:

  • Request a complete copy of your data at any time
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of optional analytics

Email hello@dayfold.app to exercise any of these rights.

Changes to this policy

We'll notify you of material changes to this policy via email or by posting a notice in the app. Your continued use of Dayfold means you accept the updated policy.

Questions?

If you have questions about how we handle your data, email hello@dayfold.app.
